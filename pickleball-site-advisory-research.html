Pickleball Site Analysis Research

This advisory provides an independent, data driven assessment of site potential for pickleball facilities, focused on understanding where demand exists, how effectively it is currently serviced, and what level of development is supportable.

The analysis is underpinned by a proprietary, purpose built pickleball database. This enables assessment of supply side variables that are not otherwise observable through standard pickleball mapping tools, including effective court availability, service capacity, and competitive intensity.

Market demand is assessed within realistic drive time catchments and considered alongside demographic demand drivers and service level benchmarks to identify areas of undersupply, emerging opportunity, or capacity pressure. Rather than relying solely on headline participation rates, the advisory translates demand into practical, site specific implications for facility scale and market positioning.
