Privacy Policy – Leisure Analytics

Last Updated: Thursday 8 January, 2026

Leisure Analytics is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines how personal information is collected, used, stored, and disclosed in accordance with the Privacy Act 1988 (Cth) and the Australian Privacy Principles (APPs).

1. Information Collected

1.1 Personal Information
Name, email address, phone number or WhatsApp number, residential suburb, business details, and identification documents provided for verification if required.

1.2 Property & Investment Information
Property addresses, budget ranges, investment criteria, risk tolerance, goals, and documents voluntarily provided such as feasibility studies, valuations, or financial models.

1.3 Automatically Collected Information
IP address, device type, browser details, pages visited, time spent on site, and cookie or analytics data.

1.4 Sensitive Information
Sensitive information is not intentionally collected unless voluntarily supplied and necessary for service delivery.

2. How Information Is Collected

Information may be collected through online forms, email, WhatsApp, phone communication, social media messages, market research processes, advisory engagements, and analytics tools such as Google Analytics or Meta Pixel.

3. Purpose of Collecting Information

Information is collected to deliver property advisory, market research and financial modelling services, prepare reports, communicate with clients, maintain business records, improve service quality, and send updates or marketing content when opted in. You may opt out of marketing at any time.

4. Storage & Security of Information

Leisure Analytics takes reasonable precautions to protect personal information from unauthorised access, loss, misuse, and interference. Information may be stored using secure cloud systems, password-protected software, encryption, and restricted staff access.

5. Disclosure of Information

Personal information may be disclosed to contractors assisting with research or reporting, technology service providers, or regulatory authorities when required by law. Information is never sold or traded. When stored outside Australia, reasonable steps are taken to ensure compliance with the Australian Privacy Principles.

6. Cookies & Analytics

Cookies and similar technologies may be used to improve user experience, track performance, and deliver targeted advertising. Cookies may be disabled in browser settings, though some features may be limited.

7. Access and Correction

You may request access to personal information held, or ask for corrections if details are inaccurate or outdated. Requests can be made using the contact details provided below.

8. Third-Party Links

External website links are not governed by this Privacy Policy, and Leisure Analytics is not responsible for the privacy practices of those sites.

9. Data Retention

Information is retained only as long as required to deliver services, maintain accurate business and compliance records, or meet legal obligations. Deletion requests may be made where legally permissible.

10. Updates to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated at any time. Any revisions will be reflected under the Last Updated date above.
